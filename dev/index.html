About Bente Sterrett

Licensed Clinical Psychologist with over 15 years of experience helping individuals find clarity, peace, and purpose.

I believe that everyone has the capacity for growth and healing. My approach is rooted in empathy, evidence-based practices, and a deep respect for your unique journey.
