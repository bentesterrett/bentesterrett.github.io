About Bente Sterrett

Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker focused on helping individuals and professionals find clarity, confidence, and purpose. My work is grounded in compassionate curiosity, evidence-informed practices, and a deep respect for each person's unique path towards growth and healing.

In my therapy practice, I support adults and adolescents in navigating anxiety, depression, complex trauma, neurodiversity, identity exploration, and life challenges. I bring a warmly logical style to our work—balancing empathy with thoughtful perspective to help you see your story with both kindness, clarity, and hope.

Alongside individual therapy, I offer clinical supervision, consultation, and trainings for professionals seeking to deepen their understanding of trauma-informed and inclusive care. Whether we're working one-on-one or in a learning setting, my goal is the same: to create space for insight, resilience, and support.

Services

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions either in-person or virtual. Using evidence-based approaches including cognitive behavioral therapy, dialectical behavioral therapy, motivational interviewing, and psychodynamic therapy.

Trainings

I offer trainings for both professionals and community members on: Trauma-Informed Care, Gender Diverse Cultural Competence, and Clinical Tools for supporting Gender Diverse Clients. Each training is tailored to your needs and audience.

Clinical Supervision

Licensing supervision for social workers in RI and MA, focused on professional development, growing clinical skills, and navigating common practice challenges as they arise.

Consultations

For licensed individuals seeking collegial input / feedback on specific situations, or broader clinical care topics.

My Approach

I integrate evidence-based/informed therapeutic modalities with a warm, intellectual, and collaborative approach that honors your unique experience.

1

Compassionate Listening and Reflection

Creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you feel truly seen and understood. Where you can deepen your understanding of self as well.

2

Evidence-based/informed methods

Utilizing a psychodynamic, person-centered approach along with cognitive behavioral therapy, dialectic behavioral therapy, and motivational interviewing.

3

Personalized Care

Creating and tracking treatment goals collaboratively to address your identified needs in a manner that best fits your unique values and circumstances.
